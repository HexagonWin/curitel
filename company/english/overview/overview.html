On the strength of our strong technical background, research is continuing into cellular/PCS/Tri-Mode phones. Similar efforts are also being made in the development of IMT-2000 and other state-of-the-art technologies in order to lead the next generation wireless communications market.

Facing the new 21st century telecommunications era, technological expertise is the next generation, Third Generation(3G) standards has to be secured. Furthermore, a pro-active "time to market" platform is being configured in order to establish an efficient product architecture, which will enable faster response to the rapidly changing market. Through future R&D efforts based on the 'select and focus' concept, the early development of 3G technologies should be achieved.
