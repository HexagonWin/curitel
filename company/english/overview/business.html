Curitel Communications Inc., started to supply CDMA handsets to Prime Co in U.S. in 1998. Curitel was the first in the world to produce handsets with the Qualcomm MSM 3000 chipset in 1999. Curitel was also the first to produce Web browser enabled handsets in Korean market.

In the mean time, Curitel has achieved certification of ISO9001 and TL9000 confirming our high technical and quality standards.

Curitel has successfully developed Tri-Mode handsets; which have resulted in accelerating sales from June of 2000. In February 2002, Curitel signed an export contract with AudioVox in U.S. to supply 5 Million units a year.

Leveraging our experience in the CDMA field, Curitel started developing GSM terminals in 2001. Curitel has obtained the European GCF certification in May 2002. From 2nd half of 2002, Curitel started supplying GSM handsets to China.

Curitel has been developing CDMA FWT products for this market since 1998. Curitel has been marketing in India, Brazil, Venezuela, and China.
