The roots of Curitel Communications, Inc. go back to the year 1983 with the
establishment of Telecom Division of Hyundai Electronics Industry Inc.
Curitel first commercialized CDMA handset in 1995 with its strong technical background.
Research has expanded into cellular PCS Tri-Mode phones. Similar efforts are also being
put in the development of IMT-2000 and other state-of-the-art technologies in order to
lead the next generation wireless communications market.

Furthermore, a pro-active "time to market" platform is being developed to establish an
efficient architecture, which will enable faster response to the rapidly changing market.
